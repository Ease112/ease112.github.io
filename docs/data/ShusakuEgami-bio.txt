Shusaku Egami received his Ph.D. degree in engineering from The 
University of Electro-Communications (UEC), Tokyo, Japan, in 2019. 
From 2018 to 2019, he received Research Fellowship for Young 
Scientists from Japan Society for the Promotion of Science. From 
2019 to 2020, He was a researcher at the Electronic Navigation 
Research Institute, Tokyo, Japan. He is currently a researcher at 
the National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and 
Technology, Japan. He is also a part-time lecturer at Hosei 
University, Tokyo, Japan, and a collaborative researcher at UEC. 
His research interests include semantic web, ontologies, and 
knowledge graphs.
